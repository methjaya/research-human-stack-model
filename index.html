About This Survey

This survey is part of an MBA research study introducing a Model - proposing that IT project success is determined by five interdependent human layers, from Technical Competency to Psychological Wellbeing. Your responses will help validate the model's core propositions.

  • All responses are completely anonymous. No personally identifying information is collected.
  • Data are used solely for academic research purposes and will be reported in aggregate form only.
  • Please answer based on your genuine professional experience. There are no right or wrong answers.
